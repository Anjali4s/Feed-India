Feed the hungry!!

FeedIndia is a unified platform which facilitates food and emergency support by connecting the govt., non-govt entities, individual donors, volunteers and the ones in need all across India.

Know someone who needs food/dry ration?

Message us on WhatsApp at +91 9113480009

You can talk to our volunteers who will verify the request, and try to help the help-seeker. We interact with government bodies, NGOs & our donors to do the needful.

Fill the short form

Give us the details about the request. It will only take 30 seconds. Our volunteers will connect with the help seeker within 24 hours.

Enter your location

Just enter your location , click on the marker to view the verified available food in your area and you can contact them directly.

Do you want to join our efforts?

Sign up as volunteer

Sign up as a volunteer and help make a difference. You can either volunteer with us directly (in resolving cases, tech management of the portal, social media management etc.), or we can get you in touch with any of our partner organisations if they need a volunteer.

Food distribution initiative

If you are an NGO, a citizen group or even an individual who is helping people with food, join hands with us. We will connect you to donors, volunteers and help seekers.

How you can help as donor?

FeedIndia does not directly accept donations. We connect you to people who are in need.
You can sign up for donating cooked food, dry ration or money.
We will assist you in reaching out to a verified person in need and in a transparent transaction.
